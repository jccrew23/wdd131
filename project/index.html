Welcome to Creative Keys!

I am Jalie Curtis. I am passionate about bringing music into any lives that I can. Music can be appreciated in so many forms, even on the piano. I believe that creativity is key to any artform and strive to develop that in my students.

I am conveniently located in Salem, VA. I provide lessons to the Greater Roanoke Area.

Private Lessons

Offering travel and in-studio lessons for ages 4-100+, for flexibility to busy lifestyles. Lessons provide enriching opportunities for pianists at every level. Very beginners can learn the fundamentals of piano playing and music reading in a supportive one on one environment to get them started on their musical journeys.
